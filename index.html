About Us

Metaverse shopping mall platform for users of various types embedded

Project META aimed to create metaverse platform for the client UPPURPLE, company that provides a solution for problems embedded in online shopping malls through a multi-complex metaverse fashion shopping platform. The main objective of the project was to create a shopping mall application with a metaverse shopping mall platform and avatars customized for users of various types embedded.

UPPURPLE is a multi-complex metaverse fashion shopping platform that aims to demolish the boundary between offline and online shopping for consumers and retail shops. Selected as a promising company in the field of new technology in the first half of 2023, UPPURPLE receives investment and infrastructural support from the Korea Institute of Startup & Entrepreneurship Development.

Teams

Data Science Team

The Data Science team played a crucial role in the project by programming a chatbot to help navigate users through the online shopping mall, developing a recommendation system that provides recommendations to customers based on their preference, and developing various interactive dashboards where customers and shop owners can observe various datasets including user activity and purchase history. Our objects are:

  • To develop a recommendation system where customers can get recommendations of clothing based on the image that they upload.
  • To develop interactive dashboards that visualizes customer information and behavior based on the information gathered from chatbot responses, shopping mall activity, and product purchase history for managers to devise customer targeted ads/events

Metaverse Dev. Team

The metaverse development team played a crucial role in the project by developing the metaverse shopping mall platform and user avatars for the metaverse. The team’s objective was to develop a user friendly metaverse shopping mall platform where online shop owners can design their own shops for users to visit, and create user avatars so that users can try on clothes of different styles and sizes. Our objects are:

  • To develop a metaverse shopping mall platform for online shopping mall owners.
  • To make saving and loading possible within the metaverse shopping mall platforms so that the settings by different shop owners can be saved and loaded freely.

Software Engineering Team

The software development team played a crucial role in the project by developing the mobile shopping mall platform, the META App. The team’s objective was to develop a user friendly shopping mall application where online shopping mall owners can register their shops and upload clothing for sale, shoppers can purchase clothing from various online shops conveniently, and to integrate the metaverse environment into the mobile application. Our objects are:

  • Front-end Development: Designing an engaging interface including the login & register page, homepage, product and detailed page, and admin page for the META mobile application.
  • Back-end Development: Develop a centralized database for registering users and adding information such as company and product information to the META mobile application.

Business Strategy & Marketing Team

The Business Strategy & Marketing team developed an online survey form for the users of the META mobile application in order to collect user information and clothing preference for future recommendations. In addition, the team created business reports that analyze the market for metaverse, as the META application aims the metaverse market. Our objects are:

  • To produce comprehensive business reports analyzing the metaverse market trends and opportunities and utilize the data for strategic decision-making.
  • Enhancing user engagement by improving the survey form for detailed user information and boost user participation in the META app.

Media Art Team

The main objective of the Media Arts team for Project META participated in creating assets for the 3D storefront of the shopping malls within the metaverse environment in the META application. The team assisted on producing the blockout and the prototype of the design, and conducted level-design concept art. The 3D storefront would enhance users’ experience while using the metaverse application. Our objects are:

  • To become skilled in using various 3D computer graphics programs such as Autodesk Maya.
  • To use 3D modeling skills in creating various 3D assets used for the Metaverse storefront, including the windows, doorframe, and cornerpieces.
