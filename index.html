About Us

Metaverse shopping mall platform for users of various types embedded

Project META aimed to create metaverse platform for the client UPPURPLE, company that provides a solution for problems embedded in online shopping malls through a multi-complex metaverse fashion shopping platform. The main objective of the project was to create a shopping mall application with a metaverse shopping mall platform and avatars customized for users of various types embedded.

UPPURPLE is a multi-complex metaverse fashion shopping platform that aims to demolish the boundary between offline and online shopping for consumers and retail shops. Selected as a promising company in the field of new technology in the first half of 2023, UPPURPLE receives investment and infrastructural support from the Korea Institute of Startup & Entrepreneurship Development and Konkuk University.
