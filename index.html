About Us

Metaverse shopping mall platform for users of various types embedded

Project META aimed to create metaverse platform for the client UPPURPLE, company that provides a solution for problems embedded in online shopping malls through a multi-complex metaverse fashion shopping platform. The main objective of the project was to create a shopping mall application with a metaverse shopping mall platform and avatars customized for users of various types embedded.

UPPURPLE is a multi-complex metaverse fashion shopping platform that aims to demolish the boundary between offline and online shopping for consumers and retail shops. Selected as a promising company in the field of new technology in the first half of 2023, UPPURPLE receives investment and infrastructural support from the Korea Institute of Startup & Entrepreneurship Development and Konkuk University.

Teams

Data Science Team

The Data Science team played a crucial role in the project by programming a chatbot to help navigate users through the online shopping mall, developing a recommendation system that provides recommendations to customers based on their preference, and developing various interactive dashboards where customers and shop owners can observe various datasets including user activity and purchase history.

Team Members:

  • Alex Kil
  • Alex Kim
  • Eric Hwang
  • Joonho Chung
  • Dongyeon Kim

Metaverse Dev. Team

The metaverse development team played a crucial role in the project by developing the metaverse shopping mall platform and user avatars for the metaverse. The team’s objective was to develop a user friendly metaverse shopping mall platform where online shop owners can design their own shops for users to visit, and create user avatars so that users can try on clothes of different styles and sizes.

Team Members:

  • Jimin Lee
  • Wonjun Lee
  • Joonyoung Moon

Software Engineering Team

The software development team played a crucial role in the project by developing the mobile shopping mall platform, the META App. The team’s objective was to develop a user friendly shopping mall application where online shopping mall owners can register their shops and upload clothing for sale, shoppers can purchase clothing from various online shops conveniently, and to integrate the metaverse environment into the mobile application.

Team Members:

  • Andrew Shin
  • Ian Kim
  • Scott Kim

Business Strategy & Marketing Team

The Business Strategy & Data Integration team developed an online survey form for the users of the META mobile application in order to collect user information and clothing preference for future recommendations. In addition, the team created business reports that analyze the market for metaverse, as the META application aims the metaverse market.

Team Members:

  • Minchan Koo
  • Singi Kim

Media Art Team

The main objective of the Media Arts team for Project META participated in creating assets for the 3D storefront of the shopping malls within the metaverse environment in the META application. The team assisted on producing the blockout and the prototype of the design, and conducted level-design concept art. The 3D storefront would enhance users’ experience while using the metaverse application.

Team Members:
